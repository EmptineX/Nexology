# Privacy Policy
- Nanoxide Development

Last Updated: 15th January, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexology (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Bot.

By using Nexology, you agree to this Privacy Policy.


---

1. Information Collected

Nexology may collect and process limited technical data required for functionality, including:

Discord Server IDs

Discord User IDs

Channel IDs

Role IDs

Bot configuration data


This data is collected only when necessary for the Bot to operate as intended.


---

2. What Nexology Does NOT Collect

Nexology does not:

Read or store private/direct messages

Collect email addresses or passwords

Track users outside of Discord

Collect personal information beyond Discord-provided identifiers



---

3. How Data Is Used

Collected data is used solely for:

Executing user-initiated commands

Maintaining server-specific configurations

Ensuring stability and functionality

Preventing abuse of the Bot


Data is never used for advertising or profiling.


---

4. Data Sharing

Nexology does not sell, trade, or share collected data with third parties.

Data may only be disclosed if required by law or valid legal request.


---

5. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely and access is restricted

Reasonable measures are taken to prevent unauthorized access

No system is guaranteed to be 100% secure


Use of the Bot is at the user’s own risk.


---

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the Bot’s operation

Server data may be deleted when the Bot is removed from a server

Certain logs may persist temporarily for stability or abuse prevention



---

7. User Responsibility

Server Owners and Administrators are responsible for:

Informing their members about the use of Nexology

Ensuring the Bot is used in compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service


The developer is not responsible for how users configure or use the Bot.


---

8. Third-Party Services

Nexology operates within Discord’s platform and is subject to Discord’s Privacy Policy.

Nexology is not responsible for Discord’s data handling practices.


---

9. Children’s Privacy

Nexology is not intended for use by individuals under the age required by Discord’s Terms of Service.

10. Changes to This Policy
